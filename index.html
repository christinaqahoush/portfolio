About

Discover the world through my lens

I am Christina Qahoush, a contemporary visual artist born in Jerusalem in 2000, currently living in Ramallah. I explore the relationship between humans and nature, focusing on Palestinian animals and plants that are disappearing due to the borders and separation from their natural habitats. Through my work, I aim to preserve these memories by using Cyanotype and collage techniques to embody the connection between nature, memory, and personal perspective.

أنا كريستينا قاحوش، فنانة فلسطينية تعمل في مجال الفنون البصرية المعاصرة. أشارك في معارض جماعية في القدس ورام الله وبيت لحم وغزة وإسبانيا، وأسعى من خلال أعمالي لتسليط الضوء على العلاقة بين الإنسان والطبيعة، والحفاظ على الكائنات النباتية والحيوانية الفلسطينية التي تختفي تدريجيًا بسبب الحدود والفصل عن بيئتها الطبيعية، باستخدام تقنيات السيانوتايب والكولاج لتجسيد الذاكرة والطبيعة من منظوري الشخصي.

Expertise

My key areas of expertise

I specialize in exploring the relationship between humans and nature through contemporary visual art, using experimental techniques to preserve memories of vanishing Palestinian flora and fauna.

Cyanotype & Collage

Expert in the Cyanotype printing process, combined with collage techniques, to create textured, memory-driven artworks that reflect nature and cultural heritage.

Contemporary Visual Arts

Proficient in creating contemporary art pieces that explore social and environmental themes, emphasizing the intersection of human experience and natural ecosystems.

Art Exhibitions & Curation

Experienced in participating in and organizing group exhibitions locally and internationally, presenting artworks that engage viewers with cultural and ecological narratives.

Research & Documentation

Conducts detailed research on Palestinian flora and fauna, documenting endangered species through artistic interpretation to preserve cultural and environmental memory.
